Why QCS

We're not just a manufacturing shop. We're a team of people who take pride in what they build. Under new ownership with fresh investment in our people, processes, and future, we're building something worth being part of.

We invest in our team. That means IPC training and certification paths for inspectors and technicians, competitive pay, a safe workplace, and an environment where your work matters and your voice is heard. We believe our people are our greatest asset — and we act like it.

What we look for

We're not looking for perfect resumes. We're looking for people who show up, own their work, and care about doing it right.

01

Ownership

You own your results — the good ones and the ones you're still working on. You don't hand problems upward. You bring solutions.

02

Integrity

You say what you mean and do what you say. You speak up when something isn't right — even when it's inconvenient.

03

Attention to detail

In our work, a wrong crimp or a missed label can cause a real problem downstream. Detail-orientation isn't optional — it's the job.

04

Willingness to grow

We invest in people who invest in themselves. IPC certification, cross-training, and advancement are available to those who want them.
