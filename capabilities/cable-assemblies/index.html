Cable Assembly Manufacturing

Cable assemblies, built your way

To your drawings. To your samples. To your deadline.

What we produce

Custom cable assemblies from simple two-end configurations to complex multi-leg assemblies. We work from customer drawings, samples, or reverse-engineered specifications — and we produce them to IPC/WHMA-A-620 workmanship standards, 100% tested before they ship.

All connector families

Capabilities in detail

  • Circular connectors (MIL-DTL-38999, MIL-C-5015, Amphenol)
  • Rectangular connectors (AMP, Molex, TE Connectivity, Deutsch)
  • USB, HDMI, Ethernet, and RF cable assemblies
  • Coax and triax assemblies
  • Power cable assemblies to 600V
  • Overmolding and strain relief

Reverse engineering from samples

No drawings? No problem. Send us a sample and we'll reverse engineer it at no charge for the first article. Once approved, reorders are fast and consistent.

Why QCS for cable assemblies

Industrial controls. Transportation. Test and measurement. RF and communications. We build the assemblies that your equipment depends on.
