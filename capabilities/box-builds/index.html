Box Build & Electro-Mechanical Assembly

Complete assemblies, ready to ship

Enclosure to final test. We handle the whole thing.

What we produce

Box builds are fully assembled electro-mechanical units: an enclosure with mounted components, internal wiring, connectors, and functional test. If you're consolidating vendors or shipping assembled subassemblies, QCS can handle the full build from enclosure to label.

Enclosure population

Capabilities in detail

  • Enclosure fabrication prep and panel layout
  • DIN rail component mounting and panel wiring
  • Internal wire harness integration
  • PCB and module installation
  • Connector and interface plate assembly
  • Final functional and hipot testing

Engineering packages

We work from your engineering packages: drawings, BOM, assembly instructions, and test procedures. No guessing, no improvisation. Your document is our work instruction.

Why QCS for box builds

Industrial controls. Automation. Test equipment. When you need one vendor to take the build from wire to finished unit — that's where we fit.

Single source for harness plus assembly

We build the wire harnesses and integrate them into the enclosure. One vendor, one point of contact, one place where the accountability lives.

Engineering-package driven

We work from your drawings, BOM, and assembly instructions. No interpretation. No surprises. You get back exactly what your documentation describes.

Tested and conforming before shipment

Every box build receives functional and hipot testing per your specification before it ships. You get a tested, conforming unit — not a unit that still needs inspection.
