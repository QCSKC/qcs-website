How we work

Four steps from first contact to conforming assemblies on your dock.

Send us your specs

Drawings, samples, or a description of what you need. We work from whatever you have and ask the right questions upfront.

Get a quote within one week

Send us your specs and we'll come back with a real, complete number — no back-and-forth just to get a starting price.

We build to your print

Every assembly is built to IPC/WHMA-A-620 and inspected to your drawing. No interpretation drift, no substitutions without your approval.

Tested and conforming — on time

100% continuity tested before it ships. You receive assemblies that meet your spec, with documentation to back it up.

IPC/WHMA-A-620 — our baseline, not our ceiling.

Every assembly we ship meets Class 2 or Class 3 workmanship standards. If it doesn't pass inspection, it doesn't leave our floor.
