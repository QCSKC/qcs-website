Who We Are

What we stand for, in writing

The principles behind every assembly we ship.

Our mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality electrical wire harnesses and cable assemblies through exceptional people, proven processes, and advanced technology. We empower our team to exceed customer expectations with every solution we provide.

Our vision

Five pillars guide how we see our future.

Industry leadership

Be recognized as a leading provider of high-quality wire harness and cable assembly solutions, known for our commitment to excellence and innovation.

Customer-centric partnerships

Build lasting partnerships with our customers by deeply understanding their needs and consistently delivering solutions that drive their success.

Empowered workforce

Foster a culture where every team member is valued, empowered, and motivated to contribute to our collective success.

Operational excellence

Continuously improve our processes and systems to achieve the highest levels of efficiency, quality, and reliability.

Technology-driven innovation

Embrace cutting-edge technologies to enhance our capabilities, improve our products, and stay ahead of industry trends.

Our core values

Eight values shape how we work, how we treat each other, and how we serve our customers.

01

Integrity first

We conduct ourselves with honesty, transparency, and respect in all our actions and relationships.

02

Commitment to quality

We hold ourselves to the highest standards in everything we do — on the floor and in the office.

03

Customer focus

Our customers' success is our success. We listen, adapt, and deliver.

04

Empowered people

We believe our team is our greatest asset. We invest in their growth, wellbeing, and success.

05

Innovative solutions

We challenge ourselves to think differently, embrace change, and find better ways to serve our customers and improve our business.

06

Agility and adaptability

We stay nimble and ready to respond to the needs of our customers and the demands of the market.

07

Accountability and pride

We own our results — the good ones and the ones we're still working on. We take pride in our work and stand behind everything we ship.

08

Safety & responsibility

We are committed to maintaining a safe workplace and acting responsibly toward our team, our community, and our environment.
