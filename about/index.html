About QCS

Built on precision. Powered by people.

A manufacturer that earns trust before it asks for business.

Our story

QCS has been producing precision wire harnesses and cable assemblies in Kansas City, Missouri for years. We've built a reputation for quality, reliability, and responsiveness among customers who need assemblies that actually conform to spec — every time, not most of the time.

Under new ownership, we're investing in our people, our processes, and our capabilities. Not because anything was broken, but because there's always a better way to serve the people who depend on us. We're building a manufacturer worth being proud of — and worth partnering with for the long term.

We're not interested in being a faceless contract shop. Every harness we ship is part of something larger. It's worth doing right.

Our mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality electrical wire harnesses and cable assemblies through exceptional people, proven processes, and advanced technology. We empower our team to exceed customer expectations with every solution we provide.
